Accommodations

Accommodations

The reunion will be held at the La Posada De Santa Fe, a one-of-a-kind resort and spa featuring Pueblo revival architecture. The resort sits on 6 acres in the heart of downtown Santa Fe, so walking anywhere is truly convenient.

Currently there is a modest block of rooms reserved; however, as reservations are made, the number of rooms will be increased depending on interest in attendance.

The price of the rooms are *$199.00 for a standard, stunning, and *$299.00, more stunning, for a suite. The phone number for the hotel and reservations is (505) 986-0000, and the event is under the name Notre Dame International. David says to contact him if there is any confusion - but rather than post his # here, post on our Facebook MMINDI Contacts page first and we'll check with him!

As of March 15, 2016, David has come up with a lower cost location approximately 10 minutes away (walking). The name of the hotel is Garrett's Desert Inn, and the phone number is (505) 982-1851. A recent quote for the time of the reunion starts at *$109.00.

* Note: this doesn't include any additional fees they may have
