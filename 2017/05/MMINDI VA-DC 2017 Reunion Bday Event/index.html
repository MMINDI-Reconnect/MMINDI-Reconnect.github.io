Updated: May 28th, 2017:

To be held in Old Town Alexandria, VA/Washington DC, on July 13-17...

OLD and NEW FRIENDS making MEMORIES

We have made some changes to our VA/DC Reunion in July 2017.

No Registration fee. No “formal” commitment - however, we would like to know if you are interesting in joining us – see Reunion website and register just to let us know you may be coming.

note Hotel Accommodations block only available at great price $169/nite until June 15th!

We would still like to go with the planned organization of the weekend as previously shared; however, it will be “pay as you go.” This means, if you think you might find yourself in the VA/DC area July 13-17 – you can meet up with us wherever we find ourselves.

This “Reunion Website” is being updated to reflect this information. Please let classmates know our new plan. We will be creating a closed Facebook page for those who will be coming to VA/DC in July so that we can share plans and ideas with each other.

We will be RETURNING all money to those that have paid anything towards the reunion. It will be returned via PayPal if this is how you paid. Those who have sent checks should have been contacted as well.
