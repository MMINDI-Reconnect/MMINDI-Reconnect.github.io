Draft Agenda

*subject to change - 2017.03.22

Tentative Schedule – Additional Details and Costs to be added as they become available.

  • As the planning of this event is in progress we’d like to ask for a $50 deposit to indicate that you plan to attend. The $50 will be deducted from your total registration. Payment can be made through PalPay to mmindi2015@gmail.com or sending a check to Lisa Thackston, 38 Forest Drive, Palmyra, Virginia 22963. This will be a family friendly event – with each event include kids if you’d like (and as you indicate they are attending). Saturday evening there will be babysitting offered during the dinner party. Please let us know if you plan to bring your family (in registration form there will be a place for this). There will be reduced costs for kids attending. Associated costs (for your planning) listed below but would/could include: travel, hotel (or house), registration fee, Friday night reception, Saturday dinner party/cash bar, Sunday BBQ, and Monday morning farewell bagels/coffee… Plus, additional things you/your family might like to do while in the area.

  • Anyone interested in helping with the planning, organizing, set-up, etc. please contact Lisa Thackston at lisathackston@mmindiconnect.com.

  • A breakdown of anticipated fees/costs are currently:
    -- Registration $35
    -- Lodging (on your own, or hotel block $169/nite, or in hub house - limited spots per night varies on room type)
    -- Friday Meet & Greet $30 adults/food/drinks, Kids (over 5yrs/under 16) $10?
    -- Saturday Dinner $90 @ Italian Restaurant w/ cash bar.
    note: working on kids prices for Dinner
    -- Sunday BBQ $25 Adults/$10 Kids under 16yr/under 5yr & Free
    -- Monday morning Coffee/Bagels $5 Adults / Kids Free

Timeline Breakdown

Thursday, July 13th
  • Check-in to a house in Old Town Alexandria – this house becomes our “hub” for the weekend. There will be a couple organized events at this location; however, it will be available to hang out/meet up, etc. throughout the weekend. It is within walking distance to the metro (to go into DC), as well as lots of shopping, food, entertainment, etc.
    The “hub” house has availability for some overnight guests. We will have a block of rooms booked at a nearby hotel. Hotel: this will be a block as soon as the location of the house is determine/reserved – the plan is for it to be within walking distance of the “hub” house.

    We can plan some specific local activities during the morning/day if folks would like – or they can do things on their own.
Saturday, July 15th, 6:00pm
  • Dinner at a local Italian Restaurant - babysitting would be offered at the “hub” house
    We can plan some specific local activities during the morning/day if folks would like – or they can do things on their own.
