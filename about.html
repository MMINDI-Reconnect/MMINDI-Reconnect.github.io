About MMI/NDI Reunion/Birthday Weekend 2017

The reunion / birthday weekend will be July 13-17, 2017 in Old Town Alexandria, VA/Washington DC. This site is for MMI and NDI former students connecting and reconnecting at the Old Town Alexandria, VA/Washington DC Reunion and celebrating (for many) our 50th birthdays.
