Accommodations

The reunion will be held at the La Posada De Santa Fe, a one-of-a-kind resort and spa featuring Pueblo revival architecture. The resort sits on 6 acres in the heart of downtown Santa Fe, so walking anywhere is truly convenient.

Currently there is a modest block of rooms reserved; however, as reservations are made, the number of rooms will be increased depending on interest in attendance.

The price of the rooms are $199.00 for a standard, stunning, and $299.00, more stunning, for a suite.

The phone number for the hotel and reservations is (505) 986-0000, and the event is under the name Notre Dame International. David says to contact him if there is any confusion - but rather than post his # here, email us @MMINDIConnect or make a note on our FaceBook group page and we'll check with him.
