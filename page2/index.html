Announcement MMINDI VA-DC 2017 Reunion/Birthday Event

To be held in Old Town Alexandria, VA/Washington DC, on July 13-17...

Hello Fellow MMI & NDI Classmates (of all years!),

Earlier this year we asked if you were interested in a 2017 Reunion/Birthday Bash (for many turning 50!) – we got a great response of interest. We asked where, when, and a couple other planning questions. We ran with the feedback we got –and then – Nothing.

So, flying with some feedback we did get - we found a Victorian Mansion to possibly host the reunion in; however, we had to let it go without enough commitment (clock was ticking on that reservation).

So we've settled on trying for a smaller 'hub' house that a few folks can stay in and the rest of us at some nearby hotel(s) that we're working on block reservations on.

To make this happen we need your donations while we secure the house and we get the rest of the details ironed out (VERY SOON - already IN PROGRESS!)

See our links for Tentative Schedule with Friday Meet & Greet, Saturday Dinner Plans, Sunday BBQ, and last Monday morning hangout! Plenty of things and places to go while we catchup and visit!
