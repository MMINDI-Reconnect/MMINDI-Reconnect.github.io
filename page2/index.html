Transportation

Santa Fe is located approximately 1 hour from Albuquerque, New Mexico. It is also possible to fly into Santa Fe; however, transportation arrangements from the Santa Fe Airport should be made ahead of time through the taxi service (505-438-0000).

There are several car rental options from the Albuquerque Airport in addition to shuttle services, and if you are adventurous you can take the Railrunner, train, from downtown Albuquerque.

If you choose to take the shuttle, the Sandia Shuttle Express operates from the Albuquerque Airport to Santa Fe throughout the day. The phone number is (888) 775-5696, and the price is $28.00, one way, and $48.00 for a round trip. The shuttle will drop you off at the La Posada in Santa Fe.

The Railrunner train service runs from Albuquerque to Santa Fe eleven times a day on weekdays*: 4:32 A.M., 5:02 A.M., 6:22 A.M., 7:19 A.M., 8:42 A.M., 9:35 A.M., 4:26 P.M., 5:34 P.M., 6:48 P.M., 7:42 P.M., and 8:38 P.M. The phone number for the Railrunner is (866) 795-RAIL (7245) and the price is $10:00 one-way. If you take the Railrunner you will have to take a cab from the Albuquerque Airport to the downtown train station, which is about 10 to 15 minutes away from the airport. When you arrive in Santa Fe, you will have to get a cab to La Posada.

Pricing and schedules are subject to change, they were correct at the time they were investigated.
