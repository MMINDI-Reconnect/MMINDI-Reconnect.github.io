Accommodations

The reunion has set a block of space at Hotel block: http://www.lorienhotelandspa.com with a link for reservation here for making the reservation online or you can use the phone number info below and say you want the "MMI/NDI Reunion" block.

The hotel is $169/night - they have a wine and cheese thing each evening and are a pet friendly hotel. You can request a pet-free/allergy-free room if needed at booking time. They have fitness 24 hours center, etc. Check out their FAQ. - John Q

Note: See addition details below from the hotel:

  • We offer valet (parking) only, which is $30 a night and allows as much in/out as your guest may need for no additional charge.
  • We offer complimentary wireless throughout the hotel as long as you are Kimpton Karma Member, which is in the link here. This will also give the chance to customize your stay (selecting pillow preferences, newspaper, and tons of other fun stuff!) and also gives you a $10 Raid the Mini Bar card. *note: $12.99 internet fee/24 hours for non-rewards members-JohnQ*
  • The reservations department is available 24 hours a day at 1-877-956-7436. All reservations should be made under the “MMI/NDI Reunion” in order to receive the group rate.

Again, the booking link is here and you can review the Kimpton Hotels Karma Rewards program here.

NOTE: Kimpton Lorien Hotel & Spa block deadline is June 15th.

Block rate: The price of the rooms are *$169.00 until the block is released and then whatever market rate is at the resort.
