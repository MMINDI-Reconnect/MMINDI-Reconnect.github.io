The Registration Form Is now open for the Santa Fe 2016 MMINDI "Connect" Reunion! (yay!) You can click on it here or on the link on the left side. Accommodations Update

Lower cost location arranged by David at the Garrett's Desert Inn within 10 minute walk (according to Google Maps).

In addition to the La Posada De Santa Fe, which is where the reunion for Friday night and our gatherings will be held, there is a place about 8-10 minutes away (walking) that David has arranged for a lower cost room solution for those willing to walk back and forth... David says: "I wanted to follow up with the name of the less expensive option for lodging in Santa Fe for the reunion. The name of the hotel is Garrett's Desert Inn, and the phone number is (505) 982-1851. A recent quote for the time of the reunion starts at $109.00".

Any issues, email us @MMINDIConnect or make a note on our FaceBook group page and we'll check with him!
