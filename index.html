Announcement MMINDI VA-DC 2017 Reunion/Birthday Event

Updated: July 10th, 2017:

To be held in Old Town Alexandria, VA/Washington DC, on July 14-17...

PLEASE TAKE A MINUTE TO READ and PASS IT ON!

OLD and NEW FRIENDS making MEMORIES

We have made some changes to our VA/DC Reunion in July 2017.

No Registration fee. No “formal” commitment - however, we would like to know if you are interesting in joining us – see Reunion website and register just to let us know you may be coming.

Are going with our planned organization of the weekend as previously shared; however, it will be “pay as you go.” This means, if you think you might find yourself in the VA/DC area July 14-17 – you can meet up with us wherever we find ourselves but you must let Lisa know you're coming because some of the plans may require/do require resevations (out of curtesy at least) for the places we're meeting.

Please see the updated AGENDA! We have plans updated (updated again) - posted on our FB Group by Lisa. If you're not in the group and want to be in the know, contact LISA or John Q. Also, the FB Group will be more up to date with things than the website after this Monday (10th) due to John Q. being on the road and not as easily able to update it (although he can try).

This “Reunion Website” is being updated to reflect this information. Please let classmates know our new plan. We have created a closed Facebook page for those who will be coming to VA/DC in July so that we can share plans and ideas with each other.

We (have) RETURNED all money to those that have paid anything towards the reunion (if you haven't gotten yours, check with LISA!).

Final note: Lisa is managing T-shirts with donated money and low cost / person for the event. It's a rush so only so many for those who were on the FB group and she'll have just a few extra available. So contact her, like, yesterday, if you haven't told her you want one.
