Registration Deposit deadline April 30 (Sunday)

The Registration Form is open for the VA-DC 2017 MMINDI "Birthday" Reunion. You can click on it here or on the link on the left side. You can see who has registered (i.e. plans to come) on Who Is Coming List.

Please note you can register now - Deposit deadline is April 30th (we need to reserve everything for the reunion). You can pay your registration fees by the Registration Payment deadline on June 25th.

Registration Details:

  • Submitting this registration will email us your information so that we can contact you about a deposit (if you haven't already made one) and payment details to be made with Lisa Thackston - she can take payment via PayPal account (use send friend money option!) or mail her a check, etc. We work with you on your total for registration. Lisa is working on pre-arranging things and needs payments to make all the reservations (event and house deposit).

  • Note: Lisa will deduct the deposit from your registration total. Also, Lisa can refund the $50 deposit if you are not able to attend, but we need deposits to make it happen! So if you think you might want to come and can help us out, make your registration and deposit.
